Outside my studies, I try to become an active part in organizations and university life. I am also a badminton fanatic, my playing schedule is <strike>non-negotiable</strike> (Unfortunately, this is not the case during the pandemic).

## Organization

### Member, Education Division of PPI Leipzig (Indonesian Student Union) (12.2018-12.2019)
As a member of the education division, I help organize tutor as well as seminar for Indonesian students in need of academic assistance. I was also the Head Organizer of Pesta Rakyat 2019 (Folk Festival) in Leipzig, to commemorate the 74 years of Indonesian Independence.

### Vice President, PPI Leipzig (12.2019-11.2020)
During my time as the Vice President, I took an active part in the day-to-day affairs of the organization. Some milestones reached by PPI Leipzig are; launching of PPI Leipzig's official website and officially registered as a student body (Arbeitsgruppe) in Leipzig University. 

### Head of Supervisory Board, PPI Leipzig (12.2020-present)
As of Desember 2020, I became the Head of the Supervisory Board in PPI Leipzig. My duties include supervision and conflict-resolution within PPI Leipzig, as needed.

I am also currently assigned as the coordinator in Leipzig, to distribute assistance from the Indonesian Embassy in Berlin.

## Badminton
I am an avid badminton fan. I follow the tournaments, I play the game, you name it. If you also happen to play badminton, let me know!
