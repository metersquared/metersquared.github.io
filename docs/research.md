## Leipzig University

### Group Member of Molecular Biophysics Group, Peter-Debye Institute for Soft Matter Physics (11.2019-11.2020)

As part of my project-oriented course, I conducted some computational work for the Molecular Biophysics group. My project involves developing a numerical method to analyze the light absorbance of a helix unfolding, namely using the exponential of the transition rate matrix. The project was supervised by Patrick Irmisch.

### Bachelor thesis student with the Quantum Statistical Physics Group, Institute of Theoretical Physics (12.2018-present)

I am currently writing my bachelor thesis with the Quantum Statistical Physics Group, involving a real-space analysis of conventional superconductors under magnetic fields.
