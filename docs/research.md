## Leipzig University

### Group Member of Molecular Biophysics Group (11.2019-11.2020)

As part of my project-oriented course, I conducted some computational work for the Molecular Biophysics group. My project involves developing a numerical method to analyze the light absorbance of a helix unfolding, namely using the exponential of the transition rate matrix. The project was supervised by Patrick Irmisch.

### Bachelor thesis student with the Quantum Statistical Physics Group (12.2018-05.2021)

I have written my bachelor thesis with the Quantum Statistical Physics Group, with the topic ["Conventional superconductors in a magnetic field: The vortex state"](https://github.com/metersquared/SCInMagField/blob/10372cbfd360009ccc24d86bbe636aba8ad39c53/FinalDraft_BachelorThesis.pdf). This thesis discusses the physical properties of a conventional superconductor when subjected to a magnetic field, under both microscopic and phenomenological viewpoints.
